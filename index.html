What Am I Looking At?

  • Top 10 Bacterial Cultures Found
    • The 10 most common bacteria strains found in the test subject's belly button. Bacteria are listed by their OTU ID numbers, and hovering over a bar shows strain names and the number of bacteria found per cluster.

    • How many times did the test subject wash their belly button each week? Ranges from 0 to 10.
  • Bacteria Cultures Per Sample
    • A comprehensive bubble chart of bacteria cultures found in the test subject's belly button. Cultures are, from left to right, ordered by OTU ID number, and the size of each bubble demonstrates the number of bacteria in each culture. Hovering over a bubble shows strain names, the OTU ID, and the number of bacteria in each cluster.
NOTE: Colors in the “Top 10 Bacteria Cultures Found” graph do not correspond with colors in the “Bacteria Cultures Per Sample” graph.
